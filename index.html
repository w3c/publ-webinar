Publishing@W3C Community Webinar

On July 27 and again on July 29 2020, publishing industry experts from the US, Europe and Asia, discussed the impressive results of the global publishing survey developed by the W3C Publishing Business Group and shared how the Publishing@W3C community is using these results to move forward in many important new areas of standards work.

Materials

See the slides presented during the webinar. Recordings of each session are included below.

Agenda

  1. Introduction: who/what is Publishing@W3C? (Tzviya Siegman)
  2. Overview of the goals of renewed Publishing@W3C activity (Cristina Mussinelli)
  3. EPUB Survey Overview and its influence on the future of the Publishing@W3C activity (Wendy Reid)
  4. Accessibility and the European Accessibility Act (Avneesh Singh)
  5. How can people participate, and keep up with our activities (Mateus Teixeira)
  6. Q & A (moderated by Tzviya Siegman, Daihei Shiohama (塩濱大平), and Shinya Takami (高見真也))
